I'm Diego, the developer of dsotodigital.
My curiosity drives me further every day. Here, I share what I've learned on an interactive platform so that you can get to know my mindset and together, we can envision fair and innovative solutions.

There's a 24hr analog clock at the top of the screen.
It's a simple way to show the time in a more interactive way.
The minutes and seconds surround the clock. Start on the `EAST` side, the `NORTH` is 1/4 of the way around the clock, and the `WEST` is 1/2 way around the clock.
On the perimeter of the hexagon, line is drawn longer with each second. The current minute is represented by a dot at the end of the counter-clockwise ticks.
As the hours pass, a new line is drawn inside of the hexagon. The first 4 hour lines appear on the upper-right segment of the clock. The next 4 appear on the upper-center segment of the clock. At noon, the first half of the day is displayed on the clock.
