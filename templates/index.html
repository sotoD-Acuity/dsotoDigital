So why the site? Well I've been learning full-stack programming on and off for about ~1.5 years (as of 6/2024) and I wanted to have something to show for it. This site's stack was picked out by another dev (Thank you, carnal!) with multiple aims:

1. It's basic but powerful: Using Linux, nginx, and python familiarizes me with many essential concepts like template rendering, event handling, devOps, cybersecurity, etc.
2. Step 1 is putting something out there, everything after that is improvement based on feedback and necessity.
3. I can express who I am, the way I want to. If I can't do it, it's only becuase I can't yet.
