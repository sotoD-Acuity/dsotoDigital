Welcome all! I'm Diego A. Soto.

Not what you were expecting? If you're reading this, chances are you've known me for a while.

On this website, you can get to know bits and piece of who I am, what I do, and how I think. That last one is the most important one.

Some people told me:"Why a website?"|"Do you even know how to code?"|"Improve your resume."|"Just use GoDaddy or something"... when I told them about how I wanted to create a presence for myself front to back. A digital one that can tell my story beyond what fits on a piece of paper, while undeniably demonstrating what I can do.

My Story? Well I'll start off by saying thank you to a long-time friend who helped me launch this site: bitmap.

Then I could get into how this website is built on a LEMP stack (Linux, NGINX, MySQL, PHP) and how each of those work with each other to render text on your screen, how somewhere in the cloud there's a computer that can show my site to anyone with an internet connection, and how much I LOVE learning about...well everything.

It doesn't end here, not even close. This is simply the beginning and I'm so excited to hit the boundaries of my abilities and go beyond. Thank you!
